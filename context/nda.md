CAPITOL COWBOYS GROUPCHAT NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA) (FULL TEXT)

Background and Scope

This NDA governs all communications across all current and historical groupchats affiliated with the Capitol Cowboys friend group and Capitol Cowboys LLC. This includes:

1. The Original 2019 Friend Group Groupchat – a pre-LLC chat where friendships, discussions, and the early culture of the group first formed.
2. The Current Capitol Cowboys LLC Member Groupchat – the primary chat for the Organization's members.
3. The Capitol Cowboys LLC Leadership Groupchat – a chat exclusively for officers, directors, or designated leaders.
4. Any Additional Groupchats or Private Chats – including temporary, project-based, or role-specific chats used to plan events, resolve issues, or execute operational tasks for the Organization.

All such chats (collectively, "Covered Communications") fall under this NDA. All members, past and present, acknowledge that: participation in any Covered Communication gives them access to confidential, sensitive, or strategic information; this participation constitutes consideration for this Agreement.

1. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

- Messages, discussions, screenshots, voice notes, media, or files shared within any Covered Communication;
- Personal information about any member or participant;
- Organizational strategy, planning, leadership deliberations, or internal disputes;
- Event planning, logistics, business decisions, or internal operations of the LLC;
- Any non-public information relating to Capitol Cowboys LLC, its business matters, or its members.

2. Obligation of Confidentiality

Members agree to:

- Treat all Covered Communication content as strictly confidential;
- Not screenshot, forward, reproduce, paraphrase, or disclose Covered Communication content to any third party without explicit permission from all individuals involved in the relevant conversation;
- Not use Covered Communication information in any way that harms, embarrasses, or disadvantages Capitol Cowboys LLC, its leadership, or any member.

3. Duration

This confidentiality obligation applies:

- During the entire time the member participates in any Covered Communication; and
- Indefinitely after a member leaves any groupchat, departs the Organization, or is removed.

4. Prohibited Conduct

The following actions constitute a breach of this NDA:

- Sharing, screenshotting, or forwarding messages outside any Covered Communication;
- Discussing internal disputes externally or with non-members;
- Revealing private, strategic, or sensitive matters disclosed by any participant;
- Using Covered Communication content for gossip, retaliation, harassment, or defamation;
- Misusing internal LLC plans, strategies, or leadership communications.

5. Damages and Remedies for Breach

Because confidentiality is essential to the integrity of the Organization, a breach of this NDA may result in consequences determined by the specific circumstances of the violation.

Capitol Cowboys LLC may seek:

- Compensatory damages, representing actual financial losses caused by the disclosure, including reputational harm, lost opportunities, and mitigation costs;
- Liquidated damages, if deemed appropriate and enforceable, provided they represent a reasonable forecast of loss and are not punitive in nature;
- Injunctive relief, including temporary or permanent court orders preventing further disclosure or misuse of Confidential Information;
- Any additional remedies permitted by law.

Members acknowledge that:

- Damages sought will be proportionate to the injury caused and consistent with applicable law;
- Remedies are intended to compensate, not punish;
- These terms constitute a reasonable and legally enforceable framework for protecting the Organization and its members.

6. Consideration

Members acknowledge that the following constitute valid consideration supporting this NDA:

- Access to the social, organizational, and cultural benefits of the Capitol Cowboys group and LLC;
- Inclusion in private chats, leadership discussions, and operational communications;
- Access to sensitive internal information, planning, and strategic coordination.

7. Acknowledgment and Acceptance

By participating in any Covered Communication or accepting membership in Capitol Cowboys LLC, the member affirms that they:

- Have read, understand, and agree to be bound by this NDA;
- Understand the consequences of violating the confidentiality requirements;
- Acknowledge that all Covered Communications fall under this NDA;
- Accept that all remedies and obligations set forth in this Agreement are binding and enforceable.
