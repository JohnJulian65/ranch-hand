CODE OF CONDUCT FOR CAPITOL COWBOYS SOCIAL CLUB (FULL TEXT)

Purpose

This Code of Conduct exists to protect the reputation, professionalism, and integrity of Capitol Cowboys LLC ("the Organization") and its members. All members agree that participation in any Capitol Cowboys activity (official or unofficial) reflects directly on the Organization.

1. Representation and Conduct

Members must conduct themselves in a manner consistent with the values, mission, and public image of Capitol Cowboys LLC in all public settings, including but not limited to:

- Social events
- Bars, restaurants, and nightlife venues
- Professional or networking environments
- Public gatherings
- Online platforms and all forms of social media

Members agree to:

- Demonstrate respect, courtesy, and professionalism toward fellow members and the public.
- Avoid behavior that could damage the reputation or credibility of the Organization.
- Adhere to all local, state, and federal laws.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy on Drugs

Capitol Cowboys LLC enforces a strict no illegal drug use or possession policy at all events, gatherings, or affiliated activities.

Engaging in illegal drug-related conduct—whether in person, online, or in any context linked to the Organization—is grounds for immediate removal from the club.

3. Social Media Conduct

Members agree that their online presence reflects on the Organization.

Accordingly, members must:

- Avoid posting defamatory, inflammatory, or inappropriate content connected to the Organization.
- Refrain from disclosing private or internal information about Capitol Cowboys LLC.
- Not use the Organization's name, branding, or affiliation in a misleading, harmful, or unauthorized manner.

4. Respect for Members and Community

Members must maintain a culture of mutual respect.

Prohibited conduct includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.
- Physical or verbal altercations.
- Any conduct that disrupts the safety or harmony of the club.

5. Compliance and Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, including:

- Verbal or written warnings
- Suspension from events
- Permanent removal from the Organization

Capitol Cowboys LLC leadership retains full discretion to determine appropriate consequences for violations.
