# Capitol Cowboys Sponsorship Program

## Event Overview

Capitol Cowboys hosts large-scale D.C. events for Washington's most connected young professionals. Events include Kentucky Derby parties, boat parties, Christmas parties, and networking mixers, with attendance ranging from 300 to 600 people per event.

## Audience Profile

- **Age Range:** 23-39
- **Industries:** Legislative and Executive branches, political consulting, public affairs, media, law, intelligence, and government contracting
- **Affiliations:** U.S. Senate and House, White House, federal agencies, national political committees, advocacy groups, and think tanks

### Attendee Breakdown

- 61.7% Government / 38.3% Private Sector
- **Government breakdown:** House 45.6%, Senate 41.8%, White House 8.7%, OGA 3.8%

## Standard Sponsorship Tiers

### Presenting Sponsor — $6,000
- Event naming rights
- Speaking opportunity
- Premium logo placement on all materials
- Attendee contact list post-event
- 8 VIP tickets

### Gold Sponsor — $3,000
- Banner at venue
- Custom co-branded activation (e.g., sponsored cocktail or lounge)
- Attendee contact list post-event
- 6 VIP tickets

### Silver Sponsor — $1,000
- Logo on check-in signage and photo wall
- Inclusion in group sponsor thank-you on social media
- 4 VIP tickets

### In-Kind Sponsor — Varies by Contribution
- Can include branded merchandise, photo booth, or food stations
- Custom benefits based on value of contribution

## Sponsorship Activation Ideas

- Branded signature cocktail
- Step-and-repeat photo wall
- VIP lounge area
- Swag station
- Giveaway or raffle
- Hashtag + QR code display

## Usage Instructions

Use this sponsorship structure when drafting outreach emails, sponsor proposals, or answering questions about partnership opportunities. Match the tier and benefits to the prospect's budget and goals, and highlight the audience demographics that are most relevant to their business.
